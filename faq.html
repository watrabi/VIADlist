What does the VIAD in VIADlist mean?

Why was this made?

We made VIADlist as an alternative to VandGD's revival-list.com.
VandGD has actively censored other revivals he didn't like and was highly biased.

Why do you not like VandGD?

VandGD attempts to control the ORC. (Old Roblox Community) exiling anyone he deems unsafe without fairly judging or investigating.

What makes a revival unsafe?

We consider an unsafe revival anything that executes unauthorized commands on your computer or anything that shares personal information

This can be anything from a R.A.T. to an "anticheat" doing file exfiltration or leaking your legal name for malicious purposes.
A revival can also be considered unsafe if the revival in question has undesirable or questionable administration. (i.e. korone and its current admins)
This can vary a little but but this is the general guideline.
